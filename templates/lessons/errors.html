Common errors

  • Remember to end every statement with a Semicolon “;”
  • If you forget a semi-colon, Dev-C++ won’t notice the Error until the next line of code.
  • “Everything that has a beginning, must have an end.” If you have an open Curly Brace ‘{‘ with no close Curly Brace ‘}’ you will get an error.
  • 95% of all errors are spelling mistakes. If you get an error, check that you have spelled the variable names correctly.
